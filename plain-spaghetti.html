How to make: Plain Spaghetti


Our spaghetti recipe features noodles cooked to perfection with a culinary technique that has been fine-tuned over generations, resulting in a dish that is both simple and sublime.

Ingredients:

  1. Dry Spaghetti Noodles

  2. Salt

  3. Water

Steps:

  1. Fill a large pot with water, add 2 tablespoons of salt and mix

  2. Place water on stove at high heat, leave until at a rolling boil

  3. Put the spaghetti noodles in the water

  4. When the noodles have softened to the perfect al dente texture, drain them using a colander

  5. Put the drained noodles on one of those pasta plates

  6. Enjoy!
