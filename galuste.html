How to make: Galuste


Experience the comfort and warmth of our signature galuste dish, lovingly crafted with the perfect balance of texture and flavor to delight your senses.

Ingredients:

  1. One egg

  2. Semolina

  3. Salt

Steps:

  1. Beat the egg in a bowl using a fork, slowly add the semolina (Make sure it's well-incorporated)

  2. Add salt to taste

  3. Let rest 10 minutes, the semolina will absorb the liquid and get fluffy

  4. Shape small dumplings using a spoon, and add them to boiling soup (Keep in mind that they will double in size)

  5. Let the galuste boil for 10 minutes, then let them sit in the soup with the stove off and the lid on for 10 more minutes

  6. Enjoy!
