How to make: Cereal with milk


A dish that transcends the mundane, our cereal with milk is a celebration of simplicity, presenting a playful contrast between the crunch of toasted grains and the velvety smoothness of our farm-fresh milk.

Ingredients:

  1. Cereal

  2. Milk

Steps:

  1. Prepare a bowl by filling it with milk

  2. Next, add the cereal into the bowl of milk

  3. Enjoy!
